Getting involved with Selenium is easy: just email us on the mailing lists or forums and we'll work with you on how to best capture your willingness to help out. OpenQA is a loose collection of QA-related projects, many focussing on Selenium, and you could get invovled with any individually project that interests you. Start with the forums, and work out what is best for you.


Ultimately, the best way to get involved is to put your money where you mouth is - by contributing code. Providing patches with your bug reports will definitely get them resolved sooner and will certainly lead to getting you on board as a developer for the project. We also greatly appreciate:

  • Help in the forums
  • Writing documentation
  • Bug reports
  • Patches
  • Feature requests
