Help from the forums

Selenium has a very active community that posts hundreds of new messages every day. The community is very diverse, ranging from entry-level testers to experienced QA engineers to hard core open source developers.

Before posting, please be sure to read the rules of the forums. Remember that everyone who works on Selenium is a volunteer, so please be respectful of their time and effort.

Selenium's forums are graciously hosted by OpenQA and available at:

http://clearspace.openqa.org/community/selenium

Professional Help

Need more professional help? No problem. We're keeping a list of companies and individuals that can provide hands-on, expert support with Selenium, continuous integration, and other QA automation techniques.

  • ThoughtWorks - the original creator of Selenium and leader in agile development.
  • BrowserMob - provides Selenium-based load testing services using thousands of real browsers.
  • Sauce Labs - cloud-based service to run Selenium tests faster and more easily.
  • Gomez - Gomez's RealityCheck product provides Selenium-in-the-cloud for all major browsers and OS's.

If you think you or your company should be listed here, just let us know by posting to the Selenium Developers forum.

IRC Chat

The Selenium team sometimes hangs out on #selenium on irc.freenode.net. You're welcome to stop by and say Hi.

This chat room is not necessarily a support chat room, so please use the forums if that's what you need.
